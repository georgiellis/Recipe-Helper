Recipe Search

to search for a recipe from ingredients, enter ingredients into the search box and you will be presented with 8 recipes using your chosen ingredients, separating each ingredient with a comma.
if a recipes ingredient is green, that means you have that ingredient in your fridge!

Once youve found a recipe you like, click the "view recipe" button to see the recipe method
